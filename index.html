Meet Lethokuhle Chivizhe (Sheisbigsteppa)

Born from a passion for fashion and a frustration with fragmented shopping experiences, WDYBI Personal Shopping was founded by Lethokuhle Chivizhe, known to her community as Sheisbigsteppa. With years of styling expertise and a keen eye for South African fashion trends, Lethokuhle recognized a gap in the market: time-poor professionals, diaspora families, and fashion enthusiasts needed a trusted curator, not another overwhelming shopping mall.

WDYBI Personal Shopping (Pty) Ltd is a registered South African company operating from Pretoria, Gauteng. Our mission is simple: transform the chaos of modern shopping into a curated, convenient experience that saves you time, money, and decision fatigue.

Our Team

WDYBI operates on an 80/20 partnership model. Lethokuhle leads all styling, client consultations, and creative direction (80%), while Manqoba handles strategy, finance, and business operations (20%). This structure ensures you always get expert fashion guidance while maintaining professional business standards.

Whether you're a busy executive who needs a wardrobe refresh, a diaspora client supporting family back home, or someone overwhelmed by SHEIN's 10,000+ daily uploads, we bring clarity, expertise, and personalized service to every interaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How do refunds work?
Service fees (consultations, runner fees) are non-refundable once service begins. For physical products, refunds follow the original retailer's policy. We facilitate returns but cannot guarantee approval. Return shipping is paid by client.
‚ùì What happens if an item doesn't fit?
We work hard to get sizing right by collecting detailed measurements. If an item doesn't fit, we can facilitate an exchange or return per the retailer's policy. You'll pay return shipping, and we may charge a small re-processing fee. To minimize sizing issues, always provide accurate measurements.
‚ùì Do you offer shipping insurance?
We can arrange courier insurance for high-value items through Aramex. Insurance costs are paid by the client. Once items leave our Pretoria hub, risk transfers to you unless insured. We strongly recommend insurance for purchases over R2,000.
‚ùì Can I cancel a service after booking?
Cancellations before service delivery begins may receive partial refunds (minus administrative fees). Once we've started shopping, sourcing, or consulting, fees are non-refundable. Tier 1 consults are always non-refundable. Please contact us within 24 hours of booking for cancellation requests.
‚ùì How long does delivery take?
Depends on courier choice. PAXI: 3-7 business days. Mukuru: 2-5 business days (domestic), varies for diaspora destinations. Aramex: 1-3 business days. International deliveries depend on customs clearance which can add 3-10 business days. We provide tracking for all shipments.

We primarily work with accessible retailers in Pretoria (Menlyn, Brooklyn Mall, Mall of Africa) and reliable online platforms (SHEIN, Superbalist, etc.).
